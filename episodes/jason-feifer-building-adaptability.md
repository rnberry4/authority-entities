## Description

Nick Berry interviews Jason Feifer, editor in chief of *Entrepreneur Magazine* and best-selling author of *Build for Tomorrow*, on adaptability in business. The conversation explores why rigid goal-setting often fails and how intentional leadership, identity, and transferable skills create resilience and long-term growth for business owners.


## Topics Covered



* Adaptability in business leadership
* Intentions versus rigid goal-setting
* Identity and leadership
* Transferable skills for long-term relevance
* Business owner mindset and growth
