## Entity Overview

Jason Feifer is an entrepreneur, author, and editor in chief of *Entrepreneur Magazine*. He specializes in adaptability in business, leadership, identity, and transferable skills that help business owners remain relevant as markets and roles evolve.

## Biography

Jason Feifer is editor in chief of *Entrepreneur Magazine* and the author of *Build for Tomorrow*. He is known for his work on business adaptability and intentional leadership, helping founders and leaders navigate change by focusing on transferable skills, identity, and long-term resilience.
